Winterstarre (Hibernation) is an exhibition of watercolors by artist and curator Anca Sinpalean at Artfoyer in FÃ¤llanden, Switzerland. Although the paintings have subjects drawn from nature and animal life, the title is a play upon a common state of mind in which us humans presently find ourselves and aims to offer some joy to visitors and passers by.

Artworks shown inside as well as in the windows of Artfoyer change on a rotational basis. They address the passers by on their way to the nearby lake of Greifensee in a visual language that is realistic, accessible and yet subtle.
